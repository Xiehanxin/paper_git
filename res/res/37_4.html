ESP-WROOM-02D and ESP-WROOM-02U are ESP8266EX-based modules developed by Espressif. Compared to ESP-WROOM-02, the RF performance of ESP-WROOM-02D and ESP-WROOM-02U are optimized. Besides, ESP-WROOM-02U integrates a U.FL connector.

The ESP8266EX integrates a Tensilica L106 32-bit RISC processor, which achieves extralow power consumption and reaches a maximum clock speed of 160 MHz. The Real-Time Operating System (RTOS) and Wi-Fi stack allow 80% of the processing power to be available for user application programming and development.ESP-WROOM-02D and ESP-WROOM-02U currently integrate a 2-MB SPI flash.

Applications:
  • Home appliances
  • Home automation
  • Smart plugs and lights
  • Industrial wireless control
  • Image Recognition
  • Baby monitors
  • IP cameras
  • Sensor networks
  • Wearable electronics
  • Wi-Fi location-aware devices
  • Security ID tags
  • Retail & Catering Applications
  • Industrial Automation
  • Smart POS Machines
Features:
  • Core: Xtensa® single-core 32-bit L106 CPU, frequency up to 160MHz
  • Memories
    • 160 KB of ROM
    • 1 KB of SRAM
    • 2,4 MB of Flash memory
  • Working Voltage: 3 V to 3.6 V
  • Up to 11 GPIOs
  • 1*10-bit ADC (up to 1 channel)
  • Communication interfaces
    • 1 I2C interfaces
    • 1 I2S interface
    • 2 SPI interfaces
    • 2 UART interfaces
  • Security:
    • 128 bit OTP
  • Extended temperature range: -40 to 85 °C
